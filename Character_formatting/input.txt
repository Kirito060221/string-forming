The principles of newspeak
/par/
Newspeak was the official language of Oceania
and had been designed to meet the ideological needs of ingsoc.
/par/
In the year 1984
there was not as yet anyone who used newspeak as his sole means of communication,
either in speech or in writing.
/break/
It was expected that newspeak would have finally superseded oldspeak
(or standard English, as we should call it) by about the year 2050.
